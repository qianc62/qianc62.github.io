About Me

My name is Chen Qian (钱忱). I am now a post-doctoral researcher (Shuimu Tsinghua Scholar Program) at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. I received a bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Institute in June 2016 and a doctoral degree from Tsinghua University in June 2021. I used to be a visiting scholar at NExT research center, National University of Singapore, Singapore, during June 2019 to June 2020. My research interests include natural language processing, business process management, data mining, information retrieval, multimodal pretraining, recommender systems, game theory, and causal inference. I have published some papers in top-tier AI- or IR-related conferences such as ACL, SIGIR, CIKM, AAAI, and IJCAI. Moreover, I have served as the program-committee member for top-tier conferences including ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, SIGIR, AAAI, and IJCAI.

Biography

Chen Qian is a Ph.D. Candidate of School of Software at Tsinghua University. His research goal is computers that can intelligently process, understand, and generate human language material. Chen Qian received the B.S. degree in software engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology, Beijing, China, in 2016. He is currently a Ph.D. student in the School of Software, Tsinghua University since August 2016. His research interests include Natural Language Processing and Business Process Management, especially on process data management, process extraction towards natural languege texts, process text generation.
